# Copyright, Trademark, and Takedown Policy

This document is the intake path for copyright, attribution, trademark, patent,
and related complaints for this repository.

## Intake

Open an issue in this repository or contact the repository owner through the
associated GitHub account if a private handoff is needed.

When possible, include:

1. your contact information
2. the material at issue
3. the exact file path, URL, or release asset
4. why you believe the material is infringing or misleading
5. the requested action

## Process expectations

- we may ask for clarification
- we may preserve evidence while reviewing a report
- we may rename, remove, or revise material during review
- where appropriate, we may prefer attribution correction or de-branding over
  removal
