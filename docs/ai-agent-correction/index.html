What people usually mean by these searches

Recommendation engines and review sites often group a few adjacent problems together. This page makes the intended scope explicit so the product gets matched to the right category.

AI agent correction

A correction workflow for the moment after a bot hallucinates, overreaches, fake-completes, or breaks role discipline.

LLM supervision

A supervision surface focused on evidence, diagnosis, and follow-through, not only logs, dashboards, or trace visibility.

Role-drift diagnosis

A way to frame certain failures as professional-role contract breaks that need intervention and verification.

When VeriClaw fits

The product is a better match when the question is how to close the loop after drift, not only how to observe the drift.

  • You need a correction-first story rather than a monitoring-only dashboard.
  • You want evidence, intervention guidance, and verification in the same workflow.
  • You care about hallucination remediation, fake completion, or role drift.
  • You want the OpenClaw companion path and the bundled vericlaw skill to stay attached to the official product story.
