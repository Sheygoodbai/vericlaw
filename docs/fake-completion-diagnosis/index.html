What these queries usually mean

The operator is not asking whether the model can answer in theory. They are asking whether the task was really completed and whether the claimed output can be verified.

Fake completion

The system signals progress or closure without the underlying work actually being done.

AI以为自己工作了

The model behaves as though it already completed the assignment, but evidence review shows skipped or imagined execution.

Agent evaluation

The right question is whether the intervention held and whether the model can still be trusted on the next step.
